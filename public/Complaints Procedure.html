Complaints Procedure

1. Reporting: Any employee who experiences or witnesses sexual harassment should report the incident promptly to their immediate reporting manager, the Human Resources department, or any designated person responsible for handling such complaints.

2. Confidentiality: All complaints will be treated with strict confidentiality, and the identity of the complainant, witnesses, and the accused will be protected to the extent possible.

3. Investigation: The company will conduct a fair and impartial investigation into all complaints of sexual harassment. The investigation will be conducted promptly, ensuring a safe environment for all parties involved.

4. Disciplinary Action: If sexual harassment is substantiated after the investigation, appropriate disciplinary action will be taken against the offender in accordance with the company's policies and applicable laws.

5. No Retaliation: The company strictly prohibits any form of retaliation against individuals who report sexual harassment or participate in the investigation process.
