Conduct Rules

[DO’S] – Every Employee Shall

1. Maintain absolute integrity at all times.

2. Maintain absolute devotion to duty at all times.

3. Maintain independence and impartiality in the discharge of duties.

4. Maintain a responsible and decent standard of conduct in private life.

5. Render prompt and courteous service to the public.

6. Observe proper decorum on and off the workplace.

7. Promptly report to superiors civil/criminal procedures against him/her, if any, in a Court of Law and the circumstances connected therewith.

8. Keep away from demonstrations organised by political parties.

9. Maintain political neutrality in their official dealings involving the company.

10. Avoid habitual indebtedness or insolvency.

11. Act in accordance with Management policies

12. Maintaining courtesy while dealing with the general public

13. Limited use of mobile phones while in the premises with an exception of official work.

 

[DON’TS] – No Employee Shall 

1. Indulge in acts detrimental to the interest of Gourmet Popcornica LLP.

2. Adopt dilatory tactics in his/her dealings with the public.

3. Practice untouchability.

4. Associate himself/herself with any banned organisations.

5. Join any association or demonstration whose objects or activities are prejudicial to the interest of the sovereignty and integrity of India, public order or morality.

6. Accept lavish or frequent hospitality from any individual, industrial or commercial firms, organisations, etc., having official dealings with him/her.

7. Lend money to or borrow money from or deposit money as a member or agent, with any person, firm or private company with whom he/she is likely to have official dealings.

8. Approach subordinates for standing surety for loans taken from private sources either by his/her, relations, or friends.

9. Appear in a public place in a state of intoxication. Indulge in any act of sexual harassment of any kind at workplace.

10. Enter into any private correspondence with Foreign Embassies or Missions/High Commissions.

11. Willful insubordination or disobedience, whether alone or in combination with others to any lawful and reasonable order of a supervisor.

12. Theft, or dishonesty in connection with the Managements’ activities or property.

13. Willful damage to or loss of the company’s goods or property.

14. Taking or giving bribes or any illegal gratification.

15. Habitual late attendance.

16. Habitual breach of any law applicable at the Management.

17. Habitual negligence or neglect of work.

18. Frequent repetition of any misconduct or omission.

19. Striking work or inciting others to strike work.

20. Contravention of the provisions of any law, or rule having the force of law.

21. Acting in a manner prejudicial to the interests of Gourmet Popcornica.

22. Drunkenness or riotous or disorderly or indecent behaviour in the premises or outside premises where such behaviour is related to or connected with the employment.

23. Gambling within the premises of the office or other place of work, where it is prohibited.

24. Smoking within the premises of the Office or other place of work, where it is prohibited.

25. Collection, without the permission of the competent authority, of any money (within the premises of the Management) except as sanctioned by any law of the land for the time being in force or rules of the Management.

26. Sleeping while on duty.

27. Watching movies, Playing video games, Browsing/Creating social media content (Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Youtube, Twitter, etc) is strictly prohibited.

28. Commission of any act which amounts to a criminal offence involving moral turpitude.

29. Absence from the employee’s appointed place of work without permission or sufficient cause.

30. Commission of any act subversive of discipline or good behaviour.

31. Willful and prolonged absence from duty without reasonable cause.

32. Failure to maintain a responsible and decent standard of conduct in private life, thereby bringing discredit to the company.

33. Having obtained an advance from the Management for a specific purpose such as building a house, purchase of conveyance, leave travel or for any other declared purpose, but not utilising the advance for the intended purpose within the period specified and diverting the money for any other use.

34. Any act unbecoming of an employee of the Management.

35. Assaulting or abusing or insulting any of the officers or other employees within the premises of office or other place of work or outside.

36. Interference in the work of other employees.

37. Approaching higher authorities directly or through other persons for promotion or any other personal favour or gain except through proper channels.

38. Writing of anonymous or pseudonymous letters criticising the Management or any other Manager/ employee of Gourmet Popcornica.

39. Spreading false rumours or giving false information or making defamatory statements (written or oral) which tend to bring the Management or its Managers into disrepute.

40. Carrying on money-lending, or any other private business.

41. Willful failure to appear before the Medical Board, when called upon to do so.

42. Abetment of or attempt at abetment of any act which amounts to misconduct.
