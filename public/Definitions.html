Definitions

1. Sexual Harassment: Sexual harassment refers to any unwelcome, unwanted, or uninvited conduct of a sexual nature that creates a hostile, intimidating, or offensive work environment. It includes, but is not limited to, the following:

2. Physical conduct: Unwanted physical contact, gestures, advances, or assault of a sexual nature.

3. Verbal conduct: Unwelcome sexual comments, jokes, innuendos, requests, or propositions.

4. Visual conduct: Displaying sexually suggestive or explicit material, including images, posters, or emails.

5. Non-verbal conduct: Making derogatory gestures, leering, stalking, or other similar behaviour of a sexual nature.
