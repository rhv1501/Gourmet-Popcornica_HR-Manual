Employee State Insurance

The Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 (ESI Act) is a social security legislation enacted by the Government of India. It provides for the establishment of the Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) to administer a comprehensive health insurance and social security scheme for employees in India.

  1. The ESI Process

a. ESI contributions will be deducted from the employee's salary on a monthly basis.

b. The current applicable ESI contribution rate will be implemented as per the ESI Act.

c. Employee ESI contributions and employer contributions will be remitted to the ESI authorities within the prescribed timelines.

  1. ESI Monthly Contribution

a. The payroll department will calculate the ESI contributions based on the employee's salary and the applicable ESI contribution rate.

b. ESI contributions will be deducted from the employee's salary on a monthly basis.

c. The employer's share of the ESI contribution will be contributed in addition to the employee's share.
