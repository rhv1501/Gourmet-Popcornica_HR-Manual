Entitlement

  1. Employees shall be entitled for laptop as per the necessity & and as approved by the HOD.
  2. Eligible employees shall be provided with laptops by the IT department immediately after joining. For this, the HR department shall intimate the IT department about new joiner’s at least one week prior to the date of joining. The IT department shall procure the same as per policy.
  3. Employees provided with laptops will not be provided additional desktops.
  4. IT Asset declaration for issue of laptop will be routed through HR/ Administration.
