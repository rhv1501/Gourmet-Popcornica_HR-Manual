IDENTITY CARD

1. Every employee will be provided with an identity card which, besides the name of the employee, will have an employee code number, photograph, and other relevant personal details.

2. Identity cards are not transferable and should be carried by the employee whenever he/she is on duty either inside or outside the premises

3. Employees shall present the identity card for inspection to any person so authorised to inspect by the Management.

4. Loss of the identity card should be immediately reported so that any possible misuse can be avoided and a replacement card issued.

5. Loss of ID card on more than 2 occasions shall be viewed as misconduct on the part of the employee and will be fined.

6. Employees are required to surrender their identity cards, badge, etc. on leaving the service. Surrender of I-card is a requirement for final settlement of dues.

7. The Management shall keep a record of all identity cards, badges issued/returned and shall carry out a surprise check of the same at least once every quarter.
