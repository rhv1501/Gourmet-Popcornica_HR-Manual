Internal Complaints Committee

1. The company will establish an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) consisting of at least four members, two employee representatives (preferably including one woman). In an ideal situation the committee will consist of:

a. Presiding Officer: Senior most female employee of the organisation

b. Member with Legal/HR Expertise: Management/ VP/Head of HR

c. Diverse Department Head: HOD of a department not related to the parties involved.

d. Employee Member:An employee who is in Executive/Deputy/Supervisory levels.

e. External Member: A member from an NGO or a similar set up.

2. The ICC will be responsible for receiving and addressing complaints of sexual harassment, conducting investigations, and recommending appropriate actions based on the findings.

3. ICC members will receive regular training on the prevention of sexual harassment, complaint handling procedures, and the legal framework surrounding sexual harassment in the workplace.
