Leave Administration

1. AII employees will be provided a Leave application every year, to be filled by the employee whenever they take leave. The balance column should be left blank to be filled by the Human Resources Department.

2. In exceptional cases(like sickness) where an employee is not able to apply for leave priorly, the said employee is expected to keep the management informed via phone or other means. Upon returning the same leave should be duly recorded based on the prescribed norms.

3. Employees are required to give one week’s notice, for 3 or more number of leave days.

4. Any employee absenting from work, except as provided for in this policy shall not only be triggered for Loss of pay(LOP) but also will be liable for disciplinary action.
