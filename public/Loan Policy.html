Loan Policy

All permanent employees of Gourmet Popcornica LLP are eligible to apply for a loan, subject to the following guidelines. The final decision to sanction the loan rests solely with the management.

Employees are encouraged to utilize this loan facility for emergencies only. It is intended to support employees during medical emergencies and  education-related expenses.

  1. Medical Emergencies

a. Employees covered under the ESI Act should provide evidence that they have not received any assistance under the act.

b. Employees not covered under the ESI Act must provide valid medical proof.

c. Submission of medical bills is mandatory to avail the loan for medical emergencies.

Employees will become eligible for a loan from Gourmet Popcornica LLP only upon completion of a continuous service period of one year without any breaks.

  1. Loan Application And Terms

a. Employees should complete the loan application form, which must be signed by a guarantor holding the same or higher grade/designation.

b. After the guarantor's signature, approval must be obtained from the departmental head before submission to the HR Department.

  1. Loan Availment Period

a. Employees can apply for loans only during the 10th to 20th of each month.

b. Consideration for loans outside this period will be based on the severity of the case, subject to individual assessment.

  1. Loan Amount

a. The loan amount shall not exceed two months' gross salary (Basic + HRA + Special Allowance) of the employee.

b. Any increase in the loan amount beyond the eligible limit requires approval from the Managing Director.

  1. Frequency And Repayment

a. Employees can avail the loan only once during the calendar year.

b. Loan repayment will be deducted from the gross salary in equal instalments, up to a maximum of six instalments.

  1. Loan Terms

a. HR may recommend loan disbursal, but the CFO/Managing Partners/Director reserve the right to modify loan terms based on risk factors or past history.

b. The Management may adjust the Loan Outstanding against Performance Bonus as and when due.

c. The recommending Head of Department (HOD) must ensure the genuineness of the loan requirement.

  1. Guarantor And Documentation

a. The same guarantor cannot act as a guarantor for any other employee or apply for a loan in their name.

b. Original documents, such as degree certificates or medical documents for medical emergencies, must be verified before loan approval.

c. A guarantor who currently has a running loan is ineligible to become a guarantor.

  1. Signing of Documents

a. Employees must sign the necessary documents as required by the Management, subject to periodic updates.
