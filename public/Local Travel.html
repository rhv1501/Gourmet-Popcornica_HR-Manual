  1. The local conveyance allowance covers all costs exclusive of toll charges and parking fees, and shall be paid on submission of the original receipts.
  2. Employee / Personal Driver using their personal vehicles must possess a valid driving license and Valid Insurance.
  3. The company will not be liable to pay for any damages that occur to the employee's personal vehicle while on official travel.
  4. All claims should be approved by the respective HOD and HR/Admin has been duly verified the reimbursement as per the policy and should be submitted within one week of the official travel. No claims will be acknowledged thereafter.
  5. Any exceptions or deviations from this policy must be approved by the respective HOD and the HR manager before such an exception on deviation is made. Any question of interpretation or applicability of any aspect of this policy will be settled by the HR Manager.
  6. The company reserves the right to add, modify, amend or withdraw the said policy with or without prior notice, at any time, at its sole discretion.
