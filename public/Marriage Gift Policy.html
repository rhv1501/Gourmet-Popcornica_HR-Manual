Marriage Gift Policy

1. Marriage Gift Policy outlines the company's approach to commemorating this special occasion in our employees' lives.

2. This policy applies to all full-time employees who have completed at least one year of continuous service with Gourmet Popcornica.

3. The policy is applicable for the first legal marriage of the employee.

4. Employees intending to avail themselves of the marriage gift must submit a written application at least one month in advance of the marriage date, along with a copy of the wedding invitation. In the absence of a wedding invitation, a declaration of the intended marriage date should be submitted.

5. Amount limits for marriage gift:

a. Management/CEO/CFO level - 10,000/-

b. Senior Manager & HOD levels - 7,500/-

c. Executives & Workers - 5000/-

6. Eligible employees will receive a congratulatory gift which may be in the form of:

a. A monetary gift amounting as per the grade.

b. A tangible gift (e.g., household appliance, luxury item) with approximate equal  value.

c. A gift voucher or certificate from a recognized retail outlet.

The form of the gift is at the company's discretion and may change from time to time.

7. Gourmet Popcornica respects the privacy of its employees. Any information provided in relation to the marriage will be treated with confidentiality and used solely for the purposes of this policy.

8. Any fraudulent application or misuse of the Marriage Gift Policy will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

9. This policy is not applicable to contract, temporary, or part-time employees.

10. While Gourmet Popcornica endeavours to celebrate the employee's happy occasions, it reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the Marriage Gift Policy at any time, with or without notice.
