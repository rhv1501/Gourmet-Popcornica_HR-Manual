On Duty

1. If On Duty is assigned during the working hours of the day, the employee has to collect the OD slip from the office reception or Admin HR which has to be duly filled by the individual and authorised by the concerned department head.

2. The OD slip(containing HOD signature) has to be submitted to the security office before leaving to mark attendance, failure to do so will be marked as leave.

3. If OD is assigned on an emergency basis to a particular employee before starting the day, the concerned Department head has to drop a mail to the HR department on the same day. And in this case, the concerned employee should fill the OD form, get in authorised by the HOD and submit to the time office/Security desk/Admin HR desk at the earliest on the next working day.

4. OD will be accounted only subject to physical verification of the OD slip.

5. Previous days’ OD slips should be submitted to the HR department before 10am on the next working day.
