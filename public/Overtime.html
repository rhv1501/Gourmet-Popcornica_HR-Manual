Overtime

The purpose of this policy is to establish clear guidelines and procedures for overtime work in accordance with relevant labour regulations. The policy aims to promote fair compensation practices and foster a healthy work-life balance for employees.

  • Overtime eligibility requires completion of regular working hours in a day.
  • Workers: Minimum 2 hours post regular working hours
  • Overtime will be paid only upon clear validation on punching history and of the productivity levels during said hours by the Head of the department/Supervisor/HR Department
  • Overtime payment will be included in the employee's monthly salary.
  • Prior approval from the reporting manager or department head is necessary for overtime work.
  • Overtime compensation will adhere to the rates specified by labour regulations.
  • Accurate records of employee overtime hours will be maintained.
  • Employees are responsible for accurately reporting their overtime hours.
  • Overtime will not be calculated if an employee misses to punch in their attendance.
  • Periodic review and updates of the policy to align with labour regulations and organisational needs.
