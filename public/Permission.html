Permission

1. In a month maximum of 4 hours permission is permitted for all categories of employees, except for factory workers.

2. This 4 hours can be utilised for across 2 or 3 occasions in a month only with no more than 2 hours in a day.(In a day maximum 2 hours)

3. This 4 hours will also include late punch-ins.

4. For factory workers in a month maximum of 2 hours permission is permitted. It can be utilised with a minimum of 30 minutes absence and maximum of 1 hour.

5. Permission slip has to be collected from the Factory time office / Admin HR, which has to be duly filled and signed by the concerned department head.

6. The filled permission slip has to be submitted to the Human Resource department on the same day without fail.

7. The permission will be valid only when it has been produced by the concerned person.

8. If HR found there is any exceeded limit of permissions are taken by any cadre of employees, which can be concluded and considered as half day leave from actual Individual's leave balance.
