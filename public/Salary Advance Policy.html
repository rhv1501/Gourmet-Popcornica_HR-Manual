Salary Advance Policy

1. All employees are eligible for an interest-free advance. Employees must have a minimum of six months of continuous service. The advance amount cannot exceed 50% of the monthly gross salary.

2. The individual's attendance will also be considered when determining their qualifying amount. The advance application must be completed in the required format and authorised by the department head before being forwarded to the HR Department.

3. Employees may petition for an advance from the 10th to the 20th of any month; emergency instances will be considered on merit.A salary advance can only be used three times in a calendar year.

4. The requested advance amount will be withheld from the employee's salary beginning the next month.

5. Only once the prior advance has been recovered can a new advance be created. Based on the individual's attendance record and other risk profile, HR would forward an application for advance to the finance department.
