Tax Compliance

The employee shall be expected to comply with tax, exchange control and other legal requirements applicable, at all times. The company reserves the right to deduct income tax at source/other statutory contributions as required by law on a monthly basis, from your salary.

  • At the commencement of each financial year, employees will receive a tax declaration form from the organisation via email.
  • The employee is required to submit the completed tax declaration form in hard copy format along with supporting documents such as investment receipts and rent receipts.
  • Tax deductions will be made based on the information provided in the declaration form.
  • Employees are expected to submit the completed form by the end of January. Failure to do so will result in actual taxes being deducted from the employee's salary.
