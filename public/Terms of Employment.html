TERMS OF EMPLOYMENT

 

1. The terms of employment for each individual will be governed by the specific details outlined in their Appointment Letter. As per company policy, individuals must be at least 18 years of age at the time of joining the company.

2. Upon appointment, it is mandatory for every employee to declare their date of birth in accordance with the supporting documentation such as an original School Leaving Certificate or Birth Certificate issued by the Municipality/Corporation. The authenticity of these documents will be verified by the company to ensure compliance.

3. This process is an important step in establishing accurate employee records and upholding legal requirements. Gourmet Popcornica LLP maintains a professional approach in confirming the age and identity of its employees to maintain transparency and adhere to all applicable regulations.
